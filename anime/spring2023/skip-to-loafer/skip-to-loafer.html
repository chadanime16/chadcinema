SKIP AND LOAFER

Synopsis

Iwakura Mitsumi, an exceptional student, has longed for a chance to depart her small town, attend a renowned university, and effect positive change globally. Nonetheless, she has concentrated so intensely on achieving her aims that she is unprepared for the distinctly unfamiliar and challenging urban existence she will encounter at a high school in Tokyo. Fortunately, she quickly befriends Shima Sousuke, a good-looking fellow classmate who is as serene as she is excessively equipped. Will this innocent rural girl succeed in bustling Tokyo with Sousuke as her companion?
