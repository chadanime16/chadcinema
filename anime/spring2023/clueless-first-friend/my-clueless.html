Synopsis

Although her classmates have given her the unflattering nickname of "Grim Reaper," fifth grader Nishimura isn't fond of it. However, the new student, Takada, finds it amusing. With Takada's cheerful but scatterbrained personality, Nishimura, who used to be bullied and isolated, begins to open up. They set out on a heartwarming and joyful summer adventure of friendship.
